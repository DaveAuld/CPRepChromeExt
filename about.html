CodeProject Reputation Watcher is an extension written for the Google Chrome browser. The extension was written as a learning exercise, and was my first attempt at an extension.
The extension also makes use of JQuery and JQueryUI, and again this was my first attempt at exploring these libraries.
This extension allows you to quickly access your statistics relating to your account. The reputation points and the associated graph are extracted from http://www.codeproject.com/ and continue to remain the copyright of "The CodeProject". This extension may stop working fully if The CodeProject change any links or source code, and I may or may not update this extension.
